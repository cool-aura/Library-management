🔍 Book Catalog & Search ▼
Browse our extensive collection of books across all genres and subjects.

📖 Book Borrowing & Returns ▼
A streamlined process to borrow and return books with real-time availability tracking.
  • Reserve books in advance from any device
  • Track your active loans and due dates at a glance
  • Automated reminders for upcoming due dates
  • Easy one-click renewal when no hold exists

Manage library memberships, roles, and permissions effortlessly.
  • Self-service registration and profile editing
  • Role-based access: member, librarian, admin
  • View full borrowing history and activity log

Transparent and automated fine calculation for overdue books.
  • Auto-calculated fines based on overdue days
  • Online payment integration for convenience
  • Fine waiver requests with librarian approval workflow

Data-driven insights to help librarians make informed decisions.
  • Most-borrowed books and trending genres
  • Member activity and engagement metrics
  • Inventory status and acquisition recommendations
  • Exportable reports in PDF and CSV formats
🔔 Notifications & Alerts ▼
Stay informed with timely notifications delivered to your preferred channel.
  • Email and in-app alerts for due dates and reservations
  • New arrival notifications matching your interests
  • Admin broadcasts and library announcements
